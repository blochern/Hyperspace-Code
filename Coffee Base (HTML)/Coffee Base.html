What is coffee?

Coffee is a drink prepared from roasted coffee beans. Coffee has a stimulating effect on humans due to its caffeine content. It's the highest selling hot drink in the world!

Things that coffee can do for humans:

  1. Increases brain activity
  2. Makes you feel refreshed and focused
  3. Increases the circulation of cortisol and adrenaline in the body

Some downsides to drinking coffee... (yes, there are downsides!)

  1. Makes it difficult to sleep
  2. You'll have to pee frequently, leading to dehydration
  3. Too much can make you anxious and jittery

Coffee is a great drink and a great tool, but it must be used responsibly!
